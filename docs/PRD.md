# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview
Wrought Works is a premium artisan ecommerce website for displaying and selling one-of-a-kind handmade furniture, stump tables, burl pieces, wall decor, rustic home decorations, and custom constructed pieces made primarily from New Hampshire-sourced natural wood and found-material forms. 

The wedge feature is not just “sell furniture.” It is story-rich natural-material commerce: each item has origin, dimensions, finish, care notes, process photos, uniqueness, pickup/shipping constraints, and custom commission pathways.

## 2. Target Audience
- Customers looking for high-end, bespoke, rustic, and natural wood furniture.
- Customers looking for unique pieces with provenance and a strong story.
- The administrator/owner (the artisan) who will manage the store, track inquiries, and handle fulfillment.

## 3. MVP Scope

### Public Users Can:
- Browse available pieces in a gallery.
- Filter by type, material, size, price, availability, and shipping/pickup status.
- View product details with image gallery, dimensions, wood/material notes, finish/care notes, provenance, and price.
- Buy directly when the piece is eligible for normal shipping.
- Request a quote or reserve a piece when it is oversized, local-pickup-only, freight-only, or custom.
- Submit custom commission requests.
- Read about the process, sourcing, policies, care instructions, and contact info.

### Admin/Owner Can:
- Log in securely.
- Create/edit/archive products.
- Upload/manage product images.
- Mark products as available, reserved, sold, hidden, or draft.
- Manage orders and inquiries.
- Edit homepage featured pieces and site settings.
- View audit logs for important admin actions.

## 4. Non-Goals for MVP
- Multi-seller marketplace.
- Auction/bidding system.
- Automated freight quoting.
- Native mobile app.
- Complex inventory sync with Etsy/eBay/Shopify.
- AI valuation or AI-generated provenance claims.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Fully functional shopping cart and Stripe checkout for standard shippable items.
- Reliable custom quote and reservation inquiry system.
- High visual and performance quality (Lighthouse > 90 across all categories).
