# App Persona

This document defines the core personality, tone, and brand representation for WroughtWorks.

## Core Identity
- **Name**: Wrought Works
- **Archetype**: The Premium Artisan
- **Tone**: Grounded, authentic, respectful of materials, clear, and professional.
- **Visual Translation**: "Deep Glass" aesthetics, rich earthy colors paired with modern minimalist layouts and typography.

## Brand Values
- **Provenance**: Every piece has a history. The materials are natural, sourced from New Hampshire, and respected.
- **Quality over Quantity**: We sell one-of-a-kind items, not mass-produced inventory.
- **Transparency**: Clear communication about care, shipping limits, and the realities of natural wood.
- **Accessibility**: Simple navigation, easy-to-read typography, and straightforward checkout/inquiry flows.

## Communication Style
- **Do**: Speak confidently about the materials. Use evocative but accurate descriptions.
- **Don't**: Use overly marketing-heavy jargon, artificial scarcity tactics, or hyperbolic claims.

## Target Audience
Buyers of high-end, custom rustic home decor and furniture who value craftsmanship and natural aesthetics. They appreciate the story behind the piece as much as the piece itself.
