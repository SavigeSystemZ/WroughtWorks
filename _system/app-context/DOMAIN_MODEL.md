## What this file is

App-specific context for this app's domain model — a universal app-context file every
AIAST app fills. Project-specific truth, derived from `PRODUCT_BRIEF.md`
and `app/`.

## Fill this in

- The core entities and what each represents.
- The key relationships and their cardinalities.
- The domain rules and invariants that must always hold.
- The units, identifiers, and lifecycle states that matter.

## Evidence that belongs here

Concrete, checkable facts — not aspirations. Never secrets, tokens, or
credentials.
