# Template MOS And Builder App Boundary

## AIAST TEMPLATE

Installable scaffold product for downstream application repos.

## MOS_TEMPLATE

Maintainer-focused meta operating system for evolving the template itself.

## Builder App

A future orchestration product that may inspect and assist template operations
but must not replace repo-local authority contracts.

## Boundary Rule

Downstream app scaffolds must not inherit MOS maintainer-only surfaces unless a
meta-system-development profile explicitly requests them.
